Figaro The Cat

All about Figaro

Figaro is also known as Figgy, Fig, Figgy Piggy, or Wiggly Figgly and he is a happy fat cat

Figaro's favourite colour is blue. Oliver knows this because he had a pen with a blue feathery bird on the top of it that Fig would always obsess over. Figgy would always find this pen no matter where it was, and play with it. So now Oliver claims that blue must be figs favourite colour. You can find fig's blue feathery bird pen here.

Meow meow meow! -Figaro
