Figaro The Cat

All about Figaro

Figaro is also known as Figgy, Fig, Figgy Piggy, or Wiggly Figgly and he is a happy fat cat

Figaro's favourite colour is blue. Oliver knows this because he had a pen with a blue feathery bird on the top of it that Fig would always obsess over. Figgy would always find this pen no matter where it was, and play with it. So now Oliver claims that blue must be figs favourite colour.

Meow meow meow! -Figaro

Figs Favourite Food

Figgy loves wet food. Especially anything with chicken in it.

Figs Favourite Toys

Some of figgy's favoutite toys include: a octopus that is meant to dangle from a doorknob, lasers pointers, and anything that envolves catnip; like his stuffed animals that are filled with it.
